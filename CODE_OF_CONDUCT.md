# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and considerate
- Giving constructive feedback
- Being open to different viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action when violations occur

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within:

- Project spaces
- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Any public interactions related to the project

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers.

All complaints will be reviewed and handled fairly.
